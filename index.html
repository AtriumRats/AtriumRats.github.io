About Us

The Atrium Rats are a grain elevation and garmet distribution company from Alaska based out of Massachusetts. We are current in Lakeland High School's Atrium, with the goal of providing Lakeland's students with high quality merchandise. We plan to expand our business beyond simply t-shirts in the near future.
